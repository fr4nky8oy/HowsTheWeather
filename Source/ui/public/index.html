How's The Weather
Double-click the globe to pick a location
What It Does

This plugin turns real-world weather into sound. Pick any location on the globe and live weather data shapes your audio through granular synthesis.

How To Use

Double-click the globe to drop a pin. Weather data modulates the sound automatically. Drag to spin. The background reflects time of day at your location. Rain, wind, and clouds appear as visual effects.

Weather Mappings

Wind controls grain size. Clouds drive density. Pressure shifts pitch. Temperature scrubs position. Humidity shapes texture. Amount scales the overall modulation depth.

Controls

Freeze (bottom-left) locks the grain buffer. Params drawer has all DSP knobs with amber arcs showing weather modulation. Amount (top-right of drawer) scales modulation depth.
